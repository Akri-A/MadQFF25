What is QFF?

Qiskit Fall Fest is a collection of quantum computing events around the world

Each Fall Fest event is organized by a local community with support from IBM Quantum Network members and Qiskit Advocates. These events are designed to be welcoming spaces for quantum enthusiasts to come together, learn, and explore the fascinating world of quantum computing.
