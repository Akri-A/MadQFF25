What is QFF?

Qiskit Fall Fest is a collection of quantum computing events around the world

Each Fall Fest event is organized by a local community with support from IBM Quantum Network members and Qiskit Advocates. These events are designed to be welcoming spaces for quantum enthusiasts to come together, learn, and explore the fascinating world of quantum computing.

Why Madrid?

Universidad Autónoma de Madrid (UAM) and CPrA (UAM Competitive Programming & Research Association) are excited to host Madrid's first Qiskit Fall Fest. Our international community of quantum computing enthusiasts, together with UAM's quantum computing initiatives, provides a welcoming environment for learning and exploring quantum technologies.

United with the growing movement of quantum computing research in Spain's public and private institutions, MadQFF'25 becomes the perfect gateway to connect the world with the expanding Spanish quantum ecosystem and the global Qiskit Fall Fest community.
